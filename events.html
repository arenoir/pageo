Pageo Lavender Farm is available throughout the year for events such as weddings, receptions, showers, and fundraisers. Please feel free to contact us regarding Packages or to make an appointment and we’ll be happy to show you around! The Pageo Wedding Packages Include:

  • Multiple options for personalized wedding ceremony and reception location
  • Set up and teardown of square reception tables and white padded chairs for up to 300 guests
  • Beautiful evening lighting that creates the perfect atmosphere for your wedding
  • Luxurious bridal room available to use hours before the ceremony
  • Indoor and outdoor commercial restrooms
  • Furnished indoor area for buffet as well as separate building for the display of the cake and/or deserts
  • Vintage “milking barn” available for groom and groomsmen
  • Large market umbrellas and space heaters to keep guests comfortable
  • Friendly onsite staff members
  • A collection of unique decor such as shepherd’s hooks, arches, and vintage tables
  • Lighted & secure on-site parking with parking attendants
  • Fun wedding games (e.g. ping pong table, 2 corn hole sets, bocce ball, and giant lawn-jinga)
  • Use of the premises to rehearse before the big day
  • Year-round access to grounds for planning
  • A comprehensive walk-through with a wedding professional to create a customized layout
  • Clean, well-manicured landscaping
  • Security and parking attendants
